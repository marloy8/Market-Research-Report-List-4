On the other hand, Helium-free PET-MRI Systems utilize alternative cooling methods, eliminating the need for helium and reducing operational costs. Both types of systems are used in neuroimaging, oncology, and research settings to diagnose and monitor various medical conditions with high precision and accuracy.</p></p>
