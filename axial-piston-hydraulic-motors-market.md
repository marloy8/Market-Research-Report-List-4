<p><p>Axial piston hydraulic motors can be classified into two main types: Swash Plate and Bent Axis. Swash Plate motors use a rotating cylinder block with angled swash plates to generate motion, while Bent Axis motors use a piston pump with a bent axis arrangement to convert fluid pressure into rotational motion. Swash Plate motors are typically more compact and have lower efficiency, while Bent Axis motors are known for their higher efficiency and greater power density. Both types are commonly used in various industries for powering machinery and equipment.</p></p>
