HQ Grade Plasmid DNA adheres to the highest quality standards and is typically used for research purposes. GMP Grade Plasmid DNA meets Good Manufacturing Practice regulations and is suitable for clinical applications. Non-GMP Grade Plasmid DNA is manufactured without adhering to GMP regulations and is typically used for preclinical research or non-clinical applications.</p></p>
