<p><p>Inflammatory heart disease encompasses several types, including atherosclerosis, myocarditis, and pericarditis. Atherosclerosis involves the buildup of plaque in the arteries, restricting blood flow to the heart. Myocarditis is inflammation of the heart muscle, often caused by a viral infection. Pericarditis is inflammation of the pericardium, the membranous sac surrounding the heart. These conditions can lead to serious complications like heart attack, heart failure, and even death if left untreated, making them important focus areas in the healthcare market.</p></p>
