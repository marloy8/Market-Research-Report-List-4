<p><p>HVDC (High-Voltage Direct Current) Converter Transformers are essential components in high-voltage direct current transmission systems. There are two main types of HVDC converter transformers: Line Commuted Converters (LCC) and Voltage Source Converters (VSC). </p><p>LCC uses thyristors to control the power flow and is typically used for long-distance transmission lines. On the other hand, VSC uses insulated gate bipolar transistors to convert AC to DC and is commonly employed for shorter distance transmission and applications requiring more flexibility and control.</p></p>
