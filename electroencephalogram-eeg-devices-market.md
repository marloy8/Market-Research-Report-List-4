<p><p>Electroencephalogram (EEG) devices market can be categorized into three main types: conventional EEG, video EEG, and dynamic EEG. Conventional EEG devices are used to monitor brain activity through electrodes placed on the scalp. Video EEG devices combine EEG with video recording for a more comprehensive analysis of brain functions. Dynamic EEG devices allow for real-time monitoring of brain activity during tasks or movements. Each type serves a unique purpose in diagnosing and monitoring various neurological conditions.</p></p>
