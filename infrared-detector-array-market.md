<p><p>Infrared detector arrays are used in various applications based on their material composition. InSb (indium antimonide) arrays offer high sensitivity and are suitable for military and surveillance applications. PbSe (lead selenide) arrays are popular in industrial and medical imaging due to their wide spectral range. PbS (lead sulfide) arrays are cost-effective options for thermal imaging. Other types of arrays include HgCdTe (mercury cadmium telluride) and QWIP (quantum well infrared photodetector), each with their own unique advantages and applications in the infrared detector array market.</p></p>
