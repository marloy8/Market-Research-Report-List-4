<p><p>Metal-base dentures are available in three main types depending on the base material used: pure titanium, titanium alloy, and cobalt-chromium alloy. Pure titanium dentures are lightweight, strong, and biocompatible, making them an excellent choice for dental prosthetics. Titanium alloy dentures offer similar benefits but with added strength and durability. Cobalt-chromium alloy dentures are known for their corrosion resistance and high tensile strength, making them ideal for patients requiring more robust prosthetics. Each type offers unique advantages based on the individual needs of patients.</p></p>
