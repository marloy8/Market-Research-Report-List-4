<p><p>Traffic sign reflective sheeting comes in two main types: glass bead type and micro prismatic type. Glass bead type sheeting uses tiny glass beads that reflect light back to the source, making the sign visible at night. Micro prismatic type sheeting uses multiple layers of prismatic shapes that reflect light at different angles, increasing visibility from various perspectives. Both types are essential for road safety, with glass bead type being more cost-effective and commonly used, while micro prismatic type offers superior reflectivity and durability.</p></p>
