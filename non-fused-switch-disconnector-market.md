<p><p>Non-fused switch disconnectors are commonly available in two main types: rotary switch disconnectors and knife-blade switch disconnectors. </p><p>Rotary switch disconnectors operate by rotating a switch handle to open or close the circuit, making them easy to use and understand. They are commonly used in industrial applications where frequent operation is required. </p><p>Knife-blade switch disconnectors function by sliding a blade into or out of a contact, providing a quick and efficient way to isolate the electrical circuit. They are often used in high voltage applications where safety and reliability are crucial.</p></p>
