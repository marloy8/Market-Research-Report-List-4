<p><p>The powered prosthetics market includes two main types: Upper Extremity Powered Prosthetics and Lower Extremity Powered Prosthetics. Upper Extremity prosthetics are designed to assist individuals with arm or hand amputations to regain functionality and dexterity. Lower Extremity prosthetics, on the other hand, are aimed at individuals with leg amputations to help them walk and move more naturally. Both types of powered prosthetics utilize advanced technology such as motors and sensors to enhance mobility and improve quality of life for amputees.</p></p>
