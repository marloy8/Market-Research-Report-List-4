<p><ul><li>Oligonucleotide RNA Microarrays (oRNA)</li><li>Complementary RNA Microarrays (cRNA)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>RNA microarrays are used for gene expression analysis, with two main types - Oligonucleotide RNA Microarrays (oRNA) and Complementary RNA Microarrays (cRNA). oRNA microarrays utilize short DNA probes that are complementary to specific RNA sequences of interest. On the other hand, cRNA microarrays utilize cDNA synthesized from target RNA samples. Both types of microarrays are powerful tools for studying gene expression levels in various biological systems, providing valuable insights into disease mechanisms and potential therapeutic targets.</p></p>
