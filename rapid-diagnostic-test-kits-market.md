<p><p>Rapid Diagnostic Test Kits market consists of various types such as Lateral Flow Test, Agglutination Assay, Immunochromatographic Assay, and Immunospot Assay. Lateral Flow Test is a type of assay that detects the presence or absence of a target analyte in a liquid sample. Agglutination Assay detects antibodies or antigens based on their ability to clump together. Immunochromatographic Assay uses a combination of antibodies and colored particles to detect the target analyte. Immunospot Assay is a type of test that measures the number of cells producing a specific protein.</p></p>
