<p><strong>The Tailgating Detection Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Imaging Measurement Tech</li><li>Non-Imaging Tech</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Tailgating detection market includes two main types: Imaging measurement technology and non-imaging technology. Imaging measurement technology uses sensors and cameras to accurately calculate the distance between individuals entering a secure area, ensuring only one person passes through at a time. Non-imaging technology, on the other hand, utilizes sensors and algorithms to detect multiple people passing through an entrance at once. Both types of technologies play a crucial role in enhancing security measures and preventing unauthorized entry into a restricted area.</p></p>
