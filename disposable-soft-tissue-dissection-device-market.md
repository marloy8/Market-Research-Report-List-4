<p><p>The market for disposable soft tissue dissection devices can be categorized into two main types: bipolar and monopolar. Bipolar devices use two electrodes to deliver controlled energy for precise tissue cutting and coagulation, while monopolar devices use a single electrode for tissue cutting and coagulation. Both types of devices are used in surgical procedures to achieve hemostasis and precise tissue dissection. The choice between bipolar and monopolar devices depends on the specific surgical needs and preferences of healthcare professionals.</p></p>
