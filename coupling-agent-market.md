<p><p>Coupling agents play a crucial role in improving the compatibility between different materials in various industries. Type I coupling agents are primarily used for enhancing adhesion between inorganic fillers and polymer matrices, while Type II coupling agents are mainly designed to improve the bond between organic polymers and inorganic substrates. The key difference lies in their specific applications and the types of materials they are meant to bond together. Both Type I and Type II coupling agents are essential for achieving optimal performance and properties in composite materials.</p></p>
