<p><p>Mineral Insulated Power Cable market comprises two main types - Single Core and Multi Core. Single core cables consist of a single conductor enclosed within a mineral insulation, commonly used for high voltage applications. Multi-core cables, on the other hand, contain multiple conductors within the same mineral insulation, offering versatility for a range of applications. Both types are known for their durability, high temperature resistance, and ability to withstand harsh environments, making them essential components in various industries.</p></p>
