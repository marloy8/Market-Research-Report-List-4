<p><p>Cheese making systems can be categorized into two main types: semi-automatic systems and fully automatic systems. Fully automatic systems, on the other hand, are more advanced and require minimal human intervention. These systems are equipped with sensors and automated controls to carry out the entire cheese-making process from start to finish. Both types of systems cater to different scales of cheese production, from small artisanal operations to large industrial facilities.</p></p>
