<p><ul><li>Cryogenic Distillation</li><li>Pressure Swing Adsorption</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Food Grade Liquid Nitrogen Market includes two main types of production methods: Cryogenic Distillation and Pressure Swing Adsorption. </p><p>Cryogenic Distillation involves the separation of nitrogen gas from air by cooling it to very low temperatures, where it can be distilled and purified. </p><p>Pressure Swing Adsorption, on the other hand, uses adsorbents to separate nitrogen from air by taking advantage of the different affinities of gases to the adsorbent material under varying pressures. </p><p>Both methods are widely used in the food industry for the production of food-grade liquid nitrogen.</p></p>
