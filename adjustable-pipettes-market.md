<p><p>Adjustable pipettes are commonly available in two main types: Single-Channel Pipettes and Multichannel Pipettes. Single-Channel Pipettes are designed to dispense liquids into a single vessel at a time, making them ideal for applications where precision and accuracy are critical. On the other hand, Multichannel Pipettes are equipped with multiple channels, allowing for simultaneous dispensing of liquids into multiple vessels. Both types of pipettes are essential tools in laboratories, research facilities, and various other industries where precise liquid handling is required.</p></p>
