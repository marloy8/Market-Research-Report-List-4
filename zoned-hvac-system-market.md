<p><p>Zoned HVAC systems use technology to divide a home or building into separate zones for more efficient heating and cooling. Duct zoning systems use dampers in ductwork to control airflow to different areas. Smart vents are connected to a central system and adjust airflow based on room occupancy or temperature. Other types of zoned HVAC systems may include wireless thermostats, sensor-based systems, or zone control panels. This variety of options allows for customized climate control in different areas of a building.</p></p>
