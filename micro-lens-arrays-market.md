<p><strong>The Micro-Lens Arrays Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Aspherical Microlens Array</li><li>Spherical Microlens Array</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Micro-Lens Arrays are categorized into two main types: Aspherical Microlens Array and Spherical Microlens Array. Aspherical Microlens Arrays have non-uniform curvature which helps in correcting aberrations and improving image quality. On the other hand, Spherical Microlens Arrays have a uniform curvature and are commonly used in light manipulation applications such as beam shaping and collimation. Both types of Micro-Lens Arrays play a crucial role in various optical systems and have specific advantages based on their design and applications.</p></p>
