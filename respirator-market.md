<p><p>Respirator Market Types can be divided into two categories: Air-purifying Respirators and Supplied-air Respirators. Air-purifying Respirators use filters or cartridges to remove contaminants from the air before it reaches the wearer, while Supplied-air Respirators provide clean air from an outside source through a hose or tube. Both types are essential for protecting workers from harmful airborne particles and gases in various industries such as healthcare, construction, and manufacturing, where exposure to hazards is common.</p></p>
