<p><p>Non-Locking Automotive Gas Spring market offers two main types: Hydraulic Damper and Dynamic Damper. Hydraulic Damper uses hydraulic fluid to provide resistance and damping force, ensuring a smooth and controlled movement. On the other hand, Dynamic Damper utilizes a mechanical system to absorb and dissipate energy, resulting in a more responsive and dynamic performance. Both types are essential in automotive applications to improve safety, comfort, and overall driving experience.</p></p>
