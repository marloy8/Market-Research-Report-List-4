<p><p>Fruit ripening systems are classified into three main market types: air control, temperature control, and other systems. Air control systems regulate the levels of oxygen, carbon dioxide, and ethylene gas in the environment to accelerate or slow down the ripening process. Temperature control systems maintain optimal temperatures to ensure proper ripening. Other market types include systems that use innovative technologies such as ethylene scrubbers or UV light to regulate ripening. Each type of system offers unique benefits for the controlled ripening of fruits.</p></p>
