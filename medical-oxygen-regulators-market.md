<p><ul><li>Single-Stage Oxygen Regulator</li><li>Multi-Stage Oxygen Regulator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Single-Stage Oxygen Regulators are designed with only one pressure reducing stage, providing a constant flow of oxygen at a fixed rate. These regulators are simple and cost-effective, suitable for basic oxygen therapy. On the other hand, Multi-Stage Oxygen Regulators have multiple pressure reducing stages, allowing for precise adjustment of oxygen flow rates. They are ideal for use in more complex medical situations where different flow rates are required for various patients or conditions.</p></p>
