<p><p>Zirconia crowns are dental prosthetics that come in two main types: layered zirconium crowns and monolithic zirconium crowns. Layered zirconium crowns consist of a strong zirconium core covered with porcelain for a natural look. On the other hand, monolithic zirconium crowns are made entirely of zirconia, offering exceptional durability and aesthetics. Both types are popular choices in the dental market due to their strength, durability, and lifelike appearance, making them ideal for restoring damaged or missing teeth.</p></p>
