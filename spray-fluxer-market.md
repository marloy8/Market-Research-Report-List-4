<p><ul><li>Stationary</li><li>Reciprocating</li><li>Standalone</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Spray fluxer market is categorized into three types: stationary, reciprocating, and standalone. Stationary spray fluxers are fixed in one position while applying flux onto the workpiece. Reciprocating spray fluxers move back and forth during the fluxing process to ensure even coverage. Standalone spray fluxers are independent machines that can be easily integrated into existing production lines. Each type offers unique benefits and capabilities, catering to various industrial needs and requirements in the fluxing process.</p></p>
