<p><p>Embolization coils are used in interventional radiology procedures to block blood flow in vessels. Pushable coils are made of platinum and are manually coiled into the desired location, offering precise placement. Detachable coils are made of platinum and polymer and can be repositioned or retrieved if necessary. Both types are effective in treating aneurysms, arterial bleeding, and other vascular abnormalities.
