<p><p>The Video Conference Phone market consists of two main types - Endpoint Systems and Multipoint Systems. Endpoint Systems refer to individual video conference phones used in smaller meeting rooms or for personal use. Multipoint Systems, on the other hand, are designed for larger conferences with multiple participants connecting from different locations. Endpoint Systems are more common for small businesses or personal use, while Multipoint Systems are ideal for large corporations or organizations with multiple offices or remote teams needing to communicate simultaneously.</p></p>
