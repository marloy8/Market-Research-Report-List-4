<p><ul><li>Forming Fabrics</li><li>Press Felts</li><li>Dryer Fabrics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Paper Machine Clothing (PMC) consists of three main types - Forming Fabrics, Press Felts, and Dryer Fabrics. Forming Fabrics are used in the forming section of the paper machine to help drain water from the pulp mixture and create a uniform sheet. Press Felts are used in the press section to remove water from the paper sheet by applying pressure. Dryer Fabrics are used in the drying section to transfer heat to the paper sheet and remove remaining moisture, resulting in a dry and finished product.</p></p>
