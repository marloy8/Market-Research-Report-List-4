<p><p>Digital dental radiography systems are classified into three main types: Ordinary Radiography Systems, Panoramic Radiography Systems, and Other market. Ordinary radiography systems are used for traditional intraoral x-rays, providing detailed images of individual teeth and surrounding tissues. Panoramic radiography systems capture a single image of the entire mouth, useful for evaluating overall oral health and diagnosing issues like impacted teeth. Other market includes emerging technologies such as cone-beam computed tomography (CBCT) systems, offering detailed 3D images for more complex dental procedures.</p></p>
