<p><p>Soil remediation encompasses various techniques to clean up contaminated soil. Bioremediation uses microbes to break down pollutants, while chemical oxidization involves treating soil with chemicals to degrade contaminants. Stabilization/solidification involves mixing additives to bind contaminants and make them less harmful. Soil washing removes contaminants through physical separation processes. Thermal desorption uses heat to volatize and remove contaminants. Each market type offers unique solutions for different types and levels of soil contamination, catering to diverse environmental remediation needs.</p></p>
