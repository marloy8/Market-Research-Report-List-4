<p><p>Fixed beam expanders are optical devices used to increase the diameter of a laser beam while preserving the beam quality. Fixed beam expanders work by using a pair of lenses to collimate the input beam and then expand it to a desired diameter.</p><p>The fixed beam expanders market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.2% during the forecast period.
