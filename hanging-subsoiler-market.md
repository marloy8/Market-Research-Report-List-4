<p><ul><li>Single-Wing</li><li>Dual-Wing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hanging subsoilers are available in two main types: Single-wing and Dual-wing. Single-wing subsoilers have a single shank that is mounted centrally on the frame, making them suitable for lighter tillage tasks. Dual-wing subsoilers have two shanks mounted on either side of the frame, allowing for deeper penetration and more aggressive tillage. Dual-wing subsoilers are typically used for tougher soil conditions and larger-scale farming operations. Both types offer effective soil loosening and compaction relief, but the choice between them depends on the specific needs of the farmer.</p></p>
