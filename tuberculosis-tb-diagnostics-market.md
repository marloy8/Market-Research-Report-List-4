<p><p>Culture-based diagnostics for tuberculosis involve growing the bacteria in a lab setting to confirm the presence of the disease. Sputum smear microscopy is a traditional method in which samples are examined under a microscope for the presence of TB bacteria. Rapid molecular diagnostics use genetic techniques to quickly detect TB bacteria in a sample. Other types of TB diagnostics may include chest X-rays, blood tests, and urine tests. Each of these methods plays a crucial role in accurately diagnosing and treating tuberculosis.</p></p>
