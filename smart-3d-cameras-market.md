<p><strong>The Smart 3D Cameras Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Binocular Stereo Vision</li><li>Time of Flight</li><li>Structured Light</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Smart 3D Cameras market includes three main types: Binocular Stereo Vision, Time of Flight, and Structured Light cameras. Binocular Stereo Vision cameras use two lenses to capture 3D images, providing accurate depth perception. Time of Flight cameras calculate distances by measuring the time it takes for light to bounce back from objects. Structured Light cameras project a pattern of light onto objects and use sensors to analyze the distortion of the pattern, allowing for precise 3D measurements.</p></p>
