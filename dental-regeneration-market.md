<p><p>Dental regeneration market encompasses various types such as dentin, enamel, pulp, and others. Dentin regeneration focuses on repairing and regenerating the hard tissue beneath the enamel, while enamel regeneration involves restoring the outer layer of the tooth. Pulp regeneration aims to restore the soft tissue inside the tooth, promoting healing and preventing the need for root canal treatment. Other types of dental regeneration may include cementum and periodontal ligament regeneration, which target tissues supporting the tooth structure.</p></p>
