<p><p>Medical stools are available in two different types - electrically adjustable and manually adjustable. Electrically adjustable stools have a mechanism that allows users to easily change the height of the stool with the push of a button. This type of stool is convenient for healthcare professionals who need to quickly adjust their seating during procedures. On the other hand, manually adjustable stools require users to physically adjust the height by turning a knob or lever. Both types of stools offer ergonomic benefits and are designed to provide comfort and support during long periods of sitting.</p></p>
