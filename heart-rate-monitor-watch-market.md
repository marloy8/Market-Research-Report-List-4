<p><ul><li>Strapless Heart Rate Monitor</li><li>Chest Strap Monitor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Heart rate monitor watches come in two main types: strapless heart rate monitors and chest strap monitors. Strapless monitors use sensors on the watch itself to track heart rate, making them convenient and comfortable to wear. Chest strap monitors, on the other hand, use a band around the chest to accurately measure heart rate during exercise. While strapless monitors are easier to wear, chest strap monitors are generally more accurate, making them popular among serious athletes and fitness enthusiasts.</p></p>
