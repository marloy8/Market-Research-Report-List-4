<p><p>Polysomnography is a type of sleep study that monitors various physiological activities during sleep. There are different types of polysomnography, including EEG (electroencephalography), which measures brain activity; dynamic polysomnography, which tracks movements during sleep; and video polysomnography, which includes video recording to monitor behavior and movements. Each type provides valuable information for diagnosing and treating sleep disorders. EEG polysomnography focuses on brain waves, dynamic polysomnography primarily tracks movements, and video polysomnography combines video recording with physiological monitoring for a comprehensive analysis of sleep patterns.</p></p>
