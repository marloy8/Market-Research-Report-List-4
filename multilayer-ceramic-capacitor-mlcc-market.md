<p><p>Multilayer Ceramic Capacitors (MLCCs) come in various types such as X7R, X5R, C0G (NP0), Y5V, and others. X7R and X5R are known for their stable capacitance over temperature and voltage variations, C0G (NP0) offer low capacitance change with temperature, Y5V provides high capacitance values but with significant variation over temperature. Other types include specialized capacitors for specific applications like high voltage or high frequency. Each type caters to different needs in terms of temperature stability, capacitance values, and voltage ratings.</p></p>
