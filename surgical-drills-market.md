<p><ul><li>Pneumatic Surgical Drills</li><li>Electric Surgical Drills</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Surgical drills are categorized into two main types: pneumatic surgical drills and electric surgical drills. Pneumatic surgical drills are powered by compressed air and are commonly used in orthopedic surgeries due to their high torque and reliability. On the other hand, electric surgical drills are powered by electricity and offer more precise control and speed regulation, making them suitable for delicate procedures such as neurosurgery. Both types of surgical drills play a crucial role in various surgical procedures, offering surgeons options based on their specific needs.</p></p>
