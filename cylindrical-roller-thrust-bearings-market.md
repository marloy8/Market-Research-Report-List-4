<p><p>Cylindrical roller thrust bearings are available with different cage materials such as steel cage and brass cage. Steel cage bearings offer high strength and durability, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications. On the other hand, brass cage bearings provide better lubrication retention and thermal conductivity. Both types serve specific purposes depending on the application requirements. Steel cage bearings are preferred in demanding environments, while brass cage bearings are ideal for applications where high speeds and temperatures are a concern.</p></p>
