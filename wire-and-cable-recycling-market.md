<p><strong>The Wire and Cable Recycling Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Manual Peeling</li><li>Mechanical Peeling</li><li>Mechanical Crushing</li><li>Chemical Method</li><li>Freezing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wire and cable recycling market involves various types of methods for processing and separating the materials. Manual peeling involves manually removing the insulation from wires, while mechanical peeling uses machines for the same purpose. Mechanical crushing involves breaking down cables into smaller pieces for recycling. Chemical methods involve using chemicals to dissolve the insulation from wires. Freezing is a less common method that freezes the insulation for easier removal. Each method has its own advantages and disadvantages in terms of efficiency and cost effectiveness in the recycling process.</p></p>
