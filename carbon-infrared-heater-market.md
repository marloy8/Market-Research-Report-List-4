<p><strong>The Carbon Infrared Heater Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Short Wave</li><li>Medium Wave</li><li>Long Wave</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Carbon infrared heaters are categorized based on the wavelength of infrared radiation they emit. Short wave heaters produce intense heat quickly, making them ideal for spot heating. Medium wave heaters offer a balance between intensity and efficiency, suitable for heating larger areas. Long wave heaters emit gentle, consistent heat over a larger area, making them ideal for heating spaces where people will be for extended periods. Each type of carbon infrared heater has its own advantages and is suited for different applications based on heating requirements.</p></p>
