<p><p>A Powered Air Purifying Respirator (PAPR) is a type of personal protective equipment used to protect the wearer from inhaling harmful particles, gases, or vapors. PAPRs are commonly used in industries such as healthcare, manufacturing, construction, and mining.</p><p>The Powered Air Purifying Respirator (PAPR) Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12.1% during the forecast period.
