<p><p>The Jewelry Synthetic Diamonds market includes two main types of synthetic diamonds: Chemical Vapor Deposition (CVD) and High Pressure High Temperature (HPHT). CVD diamonds are produced by depositing carbon atoms onto a substrate in a controlled environment, creating high-quality diamonds with minimal defects. HPHT diamonds are created by subjecting carbon to extremely high pressure and temperature, mimicking the natural process of diamond formation. Both types of synthetic diamonds are increasingly popular in the jewelry industry for their cost-effectiveness and ethical sourcing.</p></p>
