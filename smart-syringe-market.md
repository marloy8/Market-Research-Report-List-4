<p><strong>The Smart Syringe Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Auto-disable syringes</li><li>Active safety syringes</li><li>Passive safety syringes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Auto-disable syringes are designed to automatically disable after a single use, preventing accidental needlestick injuries and reuse. Active safety syringes have features such as retractable needles or sheaths that cover the needle after use, reducing the risk of needlestick injuries. Passive safety syringes have built-in safety mechanisms that require a deliberate action to activate, such as sliding shields or needle retraction. These different types of smart syringes aim to improve injection safety and reduce the spread of infections in healthcare settings.</p></p>
