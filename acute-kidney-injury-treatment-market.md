<p><p>Acute Kidney Injury (AKI) treatment market can be categorized into three types based on the underlying causes: Prerenal AKI, Postrenal AKI, and Intrinsic Renal AKI. Prerenal AKI occurs due to decreased blood flow to the kidneys, Postrenal AKI is caused by obstruction in the urinary tract, and Intrinsic Renal AKI is a result of direct damage to the kidneys themselves. Each type of AKI requires a different approach to treatment and management to prevent further kidney damage and improve outcomes.</p></p>
