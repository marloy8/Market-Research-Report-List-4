<p><p>The Wafer CMP Pads market is categorized into two main types: Hard CMP Pads and Soft CMP Pads. Hard CMP Pads are made of materials with higher durometer ratings, providing a more rigid surface for polishing semiconductor wafers. Soft CMP Pads, on the other hand, are made of materials with lower durometer ratings, offering a more flexible and conformable surface for achieving smoother finishes on wafers. Both types cater to different requirements in the semiconductor industry based on their unique properties.</p></p>
