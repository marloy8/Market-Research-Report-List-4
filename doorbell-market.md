<p><p>The doorbell market includes three main types: wired doorbells, wireless visible doorbells, and wireless invisible doorbells. Wired doorbells are connected directly to the electrical system of a home. Wireless visible doorbells use radio frequency to communicate between the doorbell button and the chime inside the house. Wireless invisible doorbells work similarly to wireless visible doorbells but have a discreet design that blends seamlessly into the home's decor. Each type offers unique features to cater to different preferences and needs in the market.</p></p>
