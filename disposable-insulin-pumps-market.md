<p><p>The Disposable Insulin Pumps market can be divided into two main types: Closed-Loop and Open-Loop systems. Closed-Loop systems, also known as automated insulin delivery systems, continuously monitor glucose levels and automatically adjust insulin delivery. On the other hand, Open-Loop systems require manual input from the user to adjust insulin doses based on glucose levels. Both types offer convenience and ease of use for diabetes patients, but Closed-Loop systems provide a more automated and precise approach to insulin delivery.</p></p>
