<p><p>Disposable latex gloves come in two main types - powdered and no powdered gloves. Powdered gloves have a light coating of cornstarch or other powder on the inside, making them easier to put on and take off. On the other hand, no powdered gloves do not have this coating and are preferred in industries where powder may contaminate products or cause allergies. Both types are widely used in industries such as healthcare, food service, cleaning, and manufacturing to provide protection against contaminants and chemicals.</p></p>
