<p><ul><li>Direct Heat Exchange</li><li>Indirect Heat Exchange</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fodder dryers are used in agriculture to dry animal feed quickly and efficiently. There are two main types of fodder dryer market: direct heat exchange and indirect heat exchange. In direct heat exchange dryers, the hot air comes into direct contact with the feed material, providing fast and effective drying. On the other hand, indirect heat exchange dryers use a separate heat exchanger to heat the air before it enters the drying chamber, making them more energy efficient and suitable for sensitive materials. Each type offers different benefits and is used in various agricultural settings.</p></p>
