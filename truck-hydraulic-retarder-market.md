<p><p>Truck hydraulic retarders are a type of braking system that helps slow down heavy vehicles by applying resistance to the drivetrain. There are two main types in the market: Parallel Hydraulic Retarders, which work independently of the vehicle's main brakes, and Tandem Hydraulic Retarders, which work in conjunction with the main brakes to provide additional stopping power. Both types are designed to improve braking performance and reduce wear on the vehicle's primary braking system, enhancing overall safety and efficiency for truck drivers.</p></p>
