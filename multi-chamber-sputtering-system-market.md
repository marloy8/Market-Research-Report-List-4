<p><ul><li>Load-lock type</li><li>Batch Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Multi Chamber Sputtering System Market can be categorized into two main types - Load-lock type and Batch Type. Load-lock type systems allow for continuous processing by loading and unloading substrates while maintaining a vacuum in the main chambers. On the other hand, Batch Type systems are designed for processing multiple substrates at once in a single batch. Both types offer advantages depending on the specific requirements of the application, leading to a diverse market for multi chamber sputtering systems.</p></p>
