<p><p>Variable Compression Ratio Actuator market can be segmented into four types. Multi-link Linkage utilizes multiple links to adjust compression ratio. Variable Cylinder Head Structure adjusts compression ratio by changing the cylinder head design. Variable Piston Top Structure alters compression ratio by adjusting the piston top shape. Variable Conrod Linkage changes compression ratio through the linkage connecting the piston and crankshaft. Each type offers unique advantages and is suitable for different applications, providing options for manufacturers to optimize engine performance and fuel efficiency.</p></p>
