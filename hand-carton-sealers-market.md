<p><p>Hand Carton Sealers market includes three main types - Manual Carton Sealers, Semi-Automatic Carton Sealers, and Fully Automatic Carton Sealers. Manual Carton Sealers require operators to manually feed and seal each carton. Semi-Automatic Carton Sealers automate the sealing process but require operators to manually adjust the machine for different box sizes. Fully Automatic Carton Sealers are the most efficient option, as they can automatically adjust to different box sizes and seal them without operator intervention. Each type offers different levels of automation and efficiency to meet various packaging needs.</p></p>
