<p><ul><li>Fixed Port</li><li>Modular</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Enterprise routers are classified into two main types: Fixed Port and Modular routers. Fixed Port routers come with a specific number of ports that cannot be expanded, making them suitable for smaller networks with consistent traffic requirements. On the other hand, Modular routers allow for flexibility and scalability by offering additional slots for modules that can be customized to meet changing network needs. This makes Modular routers ideal for larger networks that require the ability to adapt to varying traffic demands.</p></p>
