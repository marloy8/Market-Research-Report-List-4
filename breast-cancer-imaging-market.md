<p><p>Breast Cancer Imaging Market Types include Ionizing Technologies and Non-Ionizing Technologies. Ionizing Technologies such as mammography use radiation to create images of the breast tissue, allowing for early detection of cancerous cells. Non-Ionizing Technologies like ultrasound and magnetic resonance imaging (MRI) use sound waves and magnetic fields, respectively, to produce detailed images without radiation exposure. Both types of imaging play crucial roles in the diagnosis and monitoring of breast cancer, providing healthcare professionals with valuable information for treatment planning.</p></p>
