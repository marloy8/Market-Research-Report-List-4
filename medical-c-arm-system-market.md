<p><p>The Medical C Arm System market consists of two main types - CCD Mobile C-arm and FPD Mobile C-arm. CCD Mobile C-arm systems use Charge-Coupled Device technology for image detection, while FPD Mobile C-arm systems use Flat Panel Detectors. Both types of systems are utilized in medical imaging for procedures such as surgeries and interventions. The choice between CCD and FPD systems depends on factors such as image quality, cost, and overall performance needed for specific medical applications.</p></p>
