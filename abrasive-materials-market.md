<p><ul><li>Bonded</li><li>Coated</li><li>Superabrasive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The abrasive materials market can be divided into three main types: bonded abrasives, coated abrasives, and superabrasives. Bonded abrasives are composed of abrasive grains held together by a bonding material, commonly used in grinding wheels and cutting tools. Coated abrasives consist of abrasive grains coating a flexible backing material, used for sanding and polishing. Superabrasives, such as diamond and cubic boron nitride, are extremely hard materials used for precision grinding, cutting, and polishing applications in industries like aerospace and automotive.</p></p>
