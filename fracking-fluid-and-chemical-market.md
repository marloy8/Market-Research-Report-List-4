<p><p>Fracking fluids and chemicals are used in the process of hydraulic fracturing to extract oil and gas from underground rock formations. There are four main types of fracking fluids: water-based fluids, oil-based fluids, synthetic-based fluids, and foam-based fluids. Water-based fluids are the most commonly used due to their cost-effectiveness. Oil-based fluids are used for their lubricating properties. Synthetic-based fluids are environmentally-friendly alternatives. Foam-based fluids are effective in reducing friction and pressure during the fracking process.</p></p>
