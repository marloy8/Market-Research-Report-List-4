<p><p>Nitinol spring market is segmented into three main types: compression spring, extension spring, and torsion spring. Compression springs are designed to absorb and store energy when compressed, providing resistance to force. Extension springs stretch and store energy when pulled, returning to their original shape when released. Torsion springs exert rotational force when twisted, making them ideal for applications where rotational motion is required. Each type serves a specific function, catering to diverse industries such as automotive, medical devices, and aerospace.</p></p>
