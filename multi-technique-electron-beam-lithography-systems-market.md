<p><p>Multi-technique Electron Beam Lithography Systems refer to systems that combine different electron beam techniques to achieve precise patterning on substrates. Two common types of these systems are Gaussian Beam EBL Systems and Shaped Beam EBL Systems. Gaussian Beam EBL Systems use a standard Gaussian beam shape for lithography, while Shaped Beam EBL Systems utilize customized beam shapes for more complex patterning. These systems offer versatility and precision in creating nanoscale patterns for various applications in the semiconductor, photonics, and biotechnology industries.</p></p>
