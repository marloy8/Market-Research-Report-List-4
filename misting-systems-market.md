<p><p>Misting Systems market can be categorized into two main types: Low Pressure Systems and High Pressure Systems. Low Pressure Systems operate at pressures below 250 psi and are typically used for residential and small commercial applications. They are cost-effective and offer a simple installation process. On the other hand, High Pressure Systems operate at pressures above 800 psi and are ideal for large commercial or industrial settings. They provide a finer mist and are more effective at reducing temperatures in outdoor spaces.</p></p>
