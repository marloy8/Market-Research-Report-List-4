<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Nucleic Acid Testing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Transcription-mediated Amplification (TMA)</li><li>Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR)</li><li>Ligase Chain Reaction (LCR)</li><li>Whole Genome Sequencing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Nucleic Acid Testing market includes different types of testing methods for detecting genetic material. Transcription-mediated Amplification (TMA) amplifies RNA molecules to detect infections like HIV and Hepatitis. Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR) amplifies and detects DNA sequences, commonly used for diagnosing diseases and genetic disorders. Ligase Chain Reaction (LCR) identifies specific DNA sequences by linking DNA fragments together. Whole Genome Sequencing analyzes the complete DNA sequence of an organism, beneficial for studying genetic mutations and personalized medicine.</p></p>
