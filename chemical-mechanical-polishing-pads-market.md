<p><p>Chemical Mechanical Polishing (CMP) pads are used in the semiconductor industry for planarization of wafers. There are two main types of CMP pads: Hard CMP Pads and Soft CMP Pads. Hard CMP pads are made of a firmer material and are used for more aggressive polishing applications, while Soft CMP pads are made of a softer material and are used for less aggressive polishing applications. Both types of pads play a crucial role in achieving high-quality wafer planarization for semiconductor manufacturing processes.</p></p>
