<p><p>LDPE packaging is commonly used in the form of shrink films and stretch films. Shrink films are used for wrapping products tightly, providing protection during transit and storage. They shrink when heat is applied, conforming to the shape of the product. On the other hand, stretch films are used for palletizing goods, stabilizing and bundling items together. They offer elasticity and secure the items in place. Both types of packaging are popular in various industries for their cost-effectiveness and durability.</p></p>
