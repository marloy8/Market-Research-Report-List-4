Broadcast spreaders cover a larger area by distributing fertilizer evenly in a circular pattern, making them ideal for larger lawns or fields. Drop spreaders, on the other hand, provide precision by releasing fertilizer in a straight line below the spreader, making them suitable for more controlled applications.
