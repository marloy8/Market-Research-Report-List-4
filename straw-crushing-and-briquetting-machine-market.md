<p><p>Straw crushing and briquetting machines are available in three main types: mechanical, hydraulic, and screw extrusion. Mechanical machines use a mechanical press to crush and compact straw into briquettes. Hydraulic machines utilize hydraulic pressure to compress the straw into briquettes. Screw extrusion machines work by pushing the straw through a screw conveyor to compact it into briquettes. Each type of machine offers unique advantages in terms of efficiency, output quality, and cost-effectiveness in the market.</p></p>
