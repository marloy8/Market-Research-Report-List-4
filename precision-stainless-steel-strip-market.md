<p><p>Precision stainless steel strip is available in various market types, with two common options being cold drawn and hot rolled. Cold drawn strips are produced by pulling the steel through a die at room temperature, resulting in a smooth surface finish and tight dimensional tolerances. In contrast, hot rolled strips are processed at high temperatures, making them less precise but suitable for applications requiring higher strength. Both market types offer unique advantages depending on the specific requirements of the project.</p></p>
