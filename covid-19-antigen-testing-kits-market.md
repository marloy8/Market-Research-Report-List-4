<p><p>COVID-19 antigen testing kits come in two main types: PCR and qPCR. PCR (polymerase chain reaction) testing is a commonly used method that amplifies viral RNA to detect the presence of the virus. qPCR (quantitative polymerase chain reaction) is a more advanced version that provides quantitative data on the amount of viral RNA present. Both types of testing are crucial in diagnosing and monitoring COVID-19 infections, helping to identify and isolate positive cases to prevent further spread of the virus.</p></p>
