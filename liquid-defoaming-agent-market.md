<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Liquid defoaming agents can be classified into two types - Type I and Type II. Type I defoaming agents are designed to prevent foam formation during the process of production or processing, while Type II defoaming agents are used to eliminate existing foam. Type I agents are more suited for industries where foam formation is common, such as food and beverage, pharmaceutical, and chemical industries. On the other hand, Type II agents are ideal for industries that need to quickly eliminate foam buildup, such as wastewater treatment plants and coating applications.</p></p>
