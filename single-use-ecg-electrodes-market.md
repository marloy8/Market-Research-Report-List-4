<p><p>Single use ECG electrodes are available in two main types: flat paper-thin stickers and self-adhesive circular pads. The flat paper-thin stickers are designed to stick directly onto the skin and provide accurate readings during ECG monitoring. On the other hand, self-adhesive circular pads are easy to apply and remove, making them convenient for both healthcare professionals and patients. Both types of electrodes are disposable, ensuring hygiene and reducing the risk of cross-contamination during ECG procedures.</p></p>
