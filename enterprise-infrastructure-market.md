<p><p>Public, Private, and Hybrid are three types of enterprise infrastructure markets that businesses can choose from. Public infrastructure refers to services that are provided by third-party vendors and are accessible to multiple organizations on a pay-per-use basis. Private infrastructure is dedicated solely to one organization and is usually maintained on-premises or in a private cloud. Hybrid infrastructure combines elements of both public and private infrastructure, allowing companies to leverage the benefits of both. Each market type offers different levels of control, security, and scalability to suit the specific needs of the organization.</p></p>
