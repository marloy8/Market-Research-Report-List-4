<p><p>Automated Peptide Synthesizers come in two main types: single-channel and multi-channel. Single-channel synthesizers are designed to synthesize one peptide sequence at a time, making them suitable for small-scale experiments or for researchers who work with a limited number of peptides. On the other hand, multi-channel synthesizers have the capability to simultaneously synthesize multiple peptide sequences, allowing for high-throughput peptide production. This makes them ideal for labs with larger-scale peptide synthesis needs or for researchers working on multiple peptides simultaneously.</p></p>
