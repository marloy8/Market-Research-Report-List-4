<p><p>Chest compressors can be classified into two main types: mechanical and electric. Mechanical chest compressors are operated manually by compressing the chest to perform CPR. On the other hand, electric chest compressors are powered by electricity and automatically provide consistent chest compressions during CPR. Both types of chest compressors play a crucial role in emergency medical situations where high-quality CPR is needed to help maintain blood circulation and increase the chances of survival. Each type has its own set of advantages and applications based on the specific needs of the patient and healthcare provider.</p></p>
