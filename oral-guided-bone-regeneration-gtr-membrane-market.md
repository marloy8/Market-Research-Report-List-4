<p><p>Oral Guided Bone Regeneration (GTR) Membrane market consists of two main types: Absorbable Membrane and Non-absorbable Membrane. Absorbable membranes are designed to dissolve over time, allowing for the regeneration of bone tissue without the need for removal. On the other hand, Non-absorbable membranes are designed to remain in place and provide long-term support for bone regeneration. Both types of membranes play a crucial role in aiding the process of bone regeneration in oral surgery procedures.</p></p>
