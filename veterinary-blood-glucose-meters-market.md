<p><p>Veterinary blood glucose meters are available in two main types: GDH-FAD and other markets. </p><p>GDH-FAD meters use the glucose dehydrogenase flavin adenine dinucleotide enzyme to measure blood glucose levels. These meters are known for their high accuracy and reliability in glucose testing. </p><p>On the other hand, other market meters use different technologies, such as glucose oxidase or glucose hexokinase, to measure blood glucose levels. These meters may vary in terms of accuracy, speed, and ease of use compared to GDH-FAD meters.</p></p>
