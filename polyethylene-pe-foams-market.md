<p><p>Polyethylene (PE) foams are widely used for insulation, cushioning, and packaging purposes. Non-XLPE foams are conventional PE foams with closed-cell structure, offering good flexibility and shock absorption. On the other hand, XLPE foams are chemically cross-linked PE foams, providing higher strength, heat resistance, and insulation properties compared to non-XLPE foams. XLPE foams are often used in applications where higher performance is required, such as in construction, automotive, and electrical industries. Both types of PE foams play a crucial role in various industries for different purposes.</p></p>
