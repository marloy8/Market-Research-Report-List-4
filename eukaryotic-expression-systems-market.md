Expression vectors are DNA molecules used to introduce foreign genetic material into a host cell for protein expression. Competent cells are host cells that can take up foreign DNA and express it. These three components are crucial for researchers and biopharmaceutical companies to conduct experiments and produce valuable proteins in eukaryotic systems.</p></p>
