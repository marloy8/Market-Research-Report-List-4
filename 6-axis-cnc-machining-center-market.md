<p><p>Vertical type 6-Axis CNC Machining Centers have the spindle placed vertically, offering a compact design that is suitable for handling smaller workpieces. These machines are ideal for operations that require drilling and milling on different sides of a workpiece. </p><p>Horizontal type 6-Axis CNC Machining Centers have the spindle placed horizontally, allowing for more flexibility in handling larger and heavier workpieces. These machines are commonly used for high-speed machining and offer better chip evacuation.
