<p><p>Rubber asphalt distributors can be classified into two main types: drag and self-propelled. Drag distributors are typically pulled behind a truck or other vehicle and rely on the pulling force to distribute rubber asphalt efficiently. On the other hand, self-propelled distributors have their own engine and can move independently to apply rubber asphalt to surfaces. Both types of distributors are commonly used in the road construction and maintenance industry for spreading and laying rubberized asphalt materials.</p></p>
