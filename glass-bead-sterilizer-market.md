<p><p>Vertical type and Horizontal type are two common market types of Glass Bead Sterilizers. Vertical type sterilizers are designed with a vertical chamber where glass beads are heated to sterilize tools. They are suitable for smaller spaces and provide efficient sterilization. On the other hand, Horizontal type sterilizers have a horizontal chamber and are ideal for larger instruments. They offer easy access and loading of tools, making them convenient for users dealing with a higher volume of instruments. Both types of sterilizers serve the purpose of effectively sterilizing medical and dental instruments.</p></p>
