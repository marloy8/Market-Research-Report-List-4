<p><p>Waterless urinals are available in various market types such as liquid sealant cartridges, membrane traps, biological blocks, and mechanical balls. Liquid sealant cartridges contain a liquid barrier that seals off odors, while membrane traps rely on a flexible membrane to trap urine. Biological blocks contain enzymes that break down urine, and mechanical balls use a ball-and-seal mechanism to prevent odors from escaping. Each type offers a unique solution for water conservation and odor control in restroom facilities.</p></p>
