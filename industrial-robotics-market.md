<p><p>Industrial robotics market consists of five major types of robots - Articulated Robots, Parallel Robots, SCARA Robots, Cylindrical Robots, and Cartesian Robots. Articulated robots are widely used for assembly and material handling tasks. Parallel robots are ideal for high-speed applications requiring precision. SCARA robots are commonly used for pick-and-place operations in electronics manufacturing. Cylindrical robots are best suited for painting and welding tasks. Cartesian robots excel in applications requiring heavy payloads and high accuracy, such as CNC machining.</p></p>
