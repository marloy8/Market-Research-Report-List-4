<p><p>Piezoceramics market types include Lead Zinc Titanates (PZT), Lead Titanate (PT), Lead Magnesium Niobate (PMN), and others. PZT is commonly used due to its high piezoelectric properties, making it ideal for sensors, actuators, and acoustic devices. PT offers excellent electromechanical coupling capabilities, suitable for high-frequency applications. PMN provides high sensitivity and low hysteresis, making it ideal for precision instruments. Other types of piezoceramics offer unique properties that cater to specific applications in the market.</p></p>
