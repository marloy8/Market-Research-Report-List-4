<p><p>Flexible AC Transmission Systems (FACTS) Controllers market is classified into four main types based on their operation and connectivity. Series controllers are used to regulate voltage and power flow in transmission lines. Shunt controllers are used to control reactive power flow in the system. Shunt-series controllers combine both series and shunt capabilities for enhanced control. Series-series controllers are used for advanced power flow control in transmission lines. Each type of controller plays a crucial role in optimizing power transmission and grid stability.</p></p>
