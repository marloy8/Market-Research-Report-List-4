<p><p>Overhead conveyors are widely used in various industries such as automotive, food & beverage, and manufacturing. The market for overhead conveyors can be categorized into suspension chain conveyor, push type suspension chain conveyor, and drag type suspension chain conveyor. Suspension chain conveyors use a continuous loop of chain to move products along the overhead track. Push type conveyors require external force to move products, while drag type conveyors use hanging trolleys to drag products along the track. These conveyor types offer efficient and reliable transportation of materials in industrial settings.</p></p>
