Two main types of OTA updates are Firmware Over-the-Air (FOTA) and Software Over-the-Air (SOTA). FOTA updates the embedded software in devices and vehicles, ensuring they run smoothly and efficiently. SOTA updates applications and software programs, providing new features, bug fixes, and security enhancements. Both FOTA and SOTA play a crucial role in maintaining and improving the performance of IoV devices and systems.</p></p>
