<p><p>Conductive glass battery separators are available in two main market types - round and square. Round separators are typically used in cylindrical battery cells, providing a snug fit and uniform distribution of conductivity within the cell. Square separators are more commonly used in pouch or prismatic battery cells, allowing for efficient energy transfer and heat dissipation. Both market types offer high performance and reliability in various battery applications, catering to different shapes and sizes of battery designs.</p></p>
