<p><p>Missile seeker assemblies are essential components in guided missiles and play a crucial role in accurately locating and tracking targets. The three main types of missile seeker assemblies include dome, optical devices, and camera assemblies. Dome seekers utilize a transparent cover to protect the sensor from harsh environments. Optical devices incorporate lenses and mirrors to focus and capture images of targets. Camera assemblies use high-resolution cameras to visually detect and track targets. These different types of seeker assemblies cater to various defense requirements and operational needs.</p></p>
